4. Cookies and Tracking

At Little Miracles Charity, we may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience on our website. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our site. They help us recognize your browser and gather information about your preferences and activities. We use cookies to improve our website's functionality and understand how users navigate our pages. These insights enable us to make informed decisions to enhance your experience. Cookies also allow you to stay logged in and remember your preferences, making your visit more convenient. You have the option to manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Please note that if you disable cookies, some parts of our website may not function correctly. Rest assured that the information collected through cookies is used solely for analytical and functional purposes. We do not use cookies to gather personal information without your consent. Your privacy and security are important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about our use of cookies. If you have any questions about our cookie policy, please feel free to reach out to us for further information or clarification. We're here to ensure that your online experience with Little Miracles Charity is as smooth and secure as possible.
