    Yes, your donation to our charity is tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a tax receipt after making a donation, which you can use for tax purposes. Please consult your tax advisor or the relevant tax authorities for more information on deductibility based on your specific circumstances.

    Absolutely! During the donation process, you can indicate a specific program or cause you'd like your donation to support. If you have a preference, please select the appropriate option, and we'll ensure your donation goes to the area you care about most.
